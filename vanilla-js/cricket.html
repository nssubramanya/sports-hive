Cricket

Introduction

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of 11 players. The game involves scoring runs by hitting the ball and dismissing opposing players. It is played in formats like Test matches (5 days), One-Day Internationals (50 overs per side), and T20 (20 overs per side).

History

Cricket’s origins trace back to England in the 16th century. It gained prominence in the British Empire and became an international sport in the late 19th century. The first Test match was played between England and Australia in 1877.

Equipment

Cricket Bat

Usually made of willow wood.

Cricket Ball

Hard leather ball with a cork core.

Protective Gear

Pads, gloves, helmet.

Wickets

Stumps and bails.

Key Competitions

ICC Cricket World Cup

Held every four years.

Indian Premier League

A high-profile T20 league.

The Ashes

Historic rivalry series between England and Australia.

Interesting Facts

  • The longest Test match lasted 12 days (in 1939).
  • Cricket is the second-most popular sport globally, with over 2.5 billion fans.
